BRB!

by Ashley T.

2018-03-06

I will be taking a break (from posting an article, anyway) this week, but will return next Tuesday evening with an Empath 101 article!

I want to take this opportunity to thank everyone that is currently reading!

As I am not paying for any advertising, I am relying solely on word of mouth and social media to gain exposure. To block spam, I have added moderating to comments within the blog and Facebook group. The original intent is to prevent any kind of "garbage" from getting involved, and not meant to frustrate or deter any readers.

While (understandably, I get it!) it's awkward to follow along and engage when no one else appears to be, I would like to invite fans to get involved. I have seen a great amount of people that come across and like the blog, as well as my social media accounts and their posts, but see not nearly as many people are following. I only wish to bring this up in hopes of personally reaching out and saying: if my blog is of interest or help to you, please feel free to connect with me.

You can follow and stay connected on any of these social media accounts:
Facebook: www.facebook.com/empathconfessions
Instagram: www.instagram.com/empathconfessions
Pinterest board: www.pinterest.com/ashleyt/empath-confessions

A few of you have taken the opportunity to reach out to me to ask if I would offer a reading. I thank you greatly for this experience, and your reactions and feedback have helped me even more than you may know (at first, I wasn't sure if I could give a reading to someone I've never met or spoken to, but - wow)!

As I am slowly working towards launching my website for readings and coaching, I am currently doing this to gain experience. Anyone else that is intrigued can feel free to contact me (through whatever venue suits best - you can check my About Me page for my email address). My readings are purely Intuition-based, but I also use and incorporate tarot cards.

I am currently maintaining this blog (and it's social media accounts) alongside my full-time job and home (family) life. While I wouldn't dare complain about the amount of work needed to do all that, I must admit I wish I had more time to dedicate to this. It is my honest hope that as my blog grows, I can gain the experience and exposure needed to turn these hobbies (writing and giving readings) into my new full-time career. Until then, I kindly ask everyone to bare with my response time (but know that I at least follow-up and make arrangements the same day), but know that I welcome all inquiries.

In the meantime, I welcome fans to reach out personally, or at least connect, as I would like to create a relationship. I look forward to the chance to give back to you, and plan to start offering promotions to subscribers/followers in the near future!

Have a great week!
