December Update

I hope you are all settling into the holiday season with joy and gratitude!
I know I enjoy this time of year for it's comforting vibes, and the joy of giving and spending time with others. If I don't manage to gain a few pounds from the amount of baking I accomplish in this season, then I will be one small step closer to one of my New Year's resolutions! I find this to be a personal weakness; indulging beyond my upheld limits just because of living in, and enjoying, the moment... Something I let go of easily when I remind myself that I'm simply indulging in the spirit of the holidays - nothing I'm ashamed of.

While keeping busy on various projects, I do have something underway to bring my reader's attention to: I'm in the process of migrating to Blogger! Perhaps it's overdue... but my future plans see that option as being the easiest to continue this. The blog is already active here, so feel free to "move" over if you're already a subscriber.

As I'm currently wrapped up in various courses in anticipation of opening my business, the blog will continue on as something that receives bi-weekly updates. This may be more often depending on the reception at Blogger, and I welcome all comments and contact if you feel the desire to do so.

Over the last couple weeks, I've had a great response after posting the email address on Instagram for people to receive a free reading. I encourage other's to do so if they desire - by sending some basic introductory information to empathconfessions@gmail.com.
I'd like to take a moment to thank everyone I've provided a reading to so far, as your responses to them have been positively overwhelming!

More to come soon! Take care!
