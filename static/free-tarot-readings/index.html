FREE Tarot Readings!

I want to take a step back from my articles, and do something more for my readers.
I offer advice and quick readings to those that reach out, and this has been great experience for both myself and those involved. But I've also realized doing this behind the scenes isn't the only way I want to go...

While I encourage readers to continue to contact me, I would like to offer FREE Tarot Readings!

I've recently added the ability to Subscribe to the blog (look for the button near the top right of the page)... which is how I'm going to provide you with your reading!

Here's how it will work: I will email you via the address you sign up with, and will give you the opportunity to introduce yourself and let me know of area(s) of interest. I will return the results of the readings via email - complete with pictures, explanations, and any notes on thoughts I receive.

Please note that until you respond to initial "greetings" email (you can elect to only leave your name!), I will not begin your readings.

Readings will be delivered on a MONTHLY basis :)

I do have various tarot decks to choose from, and which I use for your reading will be determined by what I'm looking for.

I look forward to offering these Tarot Readings, as well as the opportunity to connect with more people!

The FREE Tarot Reading Prize is the equivalent to the paid service I provide ($10-$18 value). I do offer more in-depth, and pendulum-led, readings in paid packages. Anyone interested in purchasing my services can email me at empathconfessions@gmail.com.
