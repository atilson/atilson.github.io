About Me

I am a female in her early thirties who recently discovered that I am clairsentient and claircognizant... all thanks to meeting others that are alike.
Honestly, I'm more comfortable simply referring to myself as an Intuitive Empath.

If you have the time and curiousity, read my opening post My Discovery to learn more about how my journey unfolded.

Throughout my life I have endured rare premonition dreams, unexplanable signs of prediction, and the ability to mold into the emotions of those I was surrounded by. I never thought this to be more than my own experienced character though, and naturally saw myself fall into the personality of someone who wanted to help others because I could feel what it was like to be them.

After recognizing and researching who I am, I started to slowly notice everything I pick up on (what emotions came from who, what thoughts I received while observing and conversing with someone). Before this point, these "perceptions" mostly turned into me having conflicted thoughts, unexplained mood shifts, and health issues. Additionally, and most noteworthy for me, it also resulted in me one day asking myself who I was beneath everything I've taken on.

After over a year on this journey, I wanted to share some of my experience in a blog in hope that someone will find some comfort, guidance, understanding, insight or even just enjoy as a curious read. After time spent working on myself, I felt an urge to write and subsequently came up with a list of ideas to base articles on.

As of April 2018, I have moved off of a weekly posting schedule and focus my time on providing Tarot Readings. I still have great plans for Empath Confessions, and hope you check back to see more as time goes on.

I welcome all contact by email.

I welcome and thank all for stopping by!

Namaste!
