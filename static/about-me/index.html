About Me

I am a female in her early thirties who recently discovered I am an empath/clairsentient, and claircognizant... all thanks to meeting others that are alike. Honestly, I'm currently more comfortable simply referring to myself as an Intuitive Empath.

If you have the time and curiousity, read my opening post My Discovery to learn more about how my journey unfolded.

Throughout my life I have endured rare premonition dreams, unexplanable signs of prediction, and the ability to mold into the emotions of those I was surrounded by. I never thought this to be more than my own experienced character though, and naturally saw myself fall into the personality of someone who wanted to help others because I felt what it was like.

After recognizing and researching who I am, I started to slowly notice everything I pick up on (what emotions came from who, what thoughts I received while observing and conversing with someone). Before this point, these "perceptions" mostly turned into me having conflicted thoughts, unexplained mood shifts, and health issues.

After over a year on this journey, I wanted to share some of my experience in a blog in hope that someone will find some comfort, guidance, understanding, insight or even just enjoy as a curious read. After time spent working on myself, I felt an urge to write and subsequently came up with a list of ideas to base articles on.

I welcome contact by email.

I welcome and thank all for stopping by! Remember that I publish a new article every Tuesday!

Namaste!
