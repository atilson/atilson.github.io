Summer Update

Greetings friends!
I'm happy to provide an update on what's to come in the near future for Empath Confessions!

As you may have seen through the social media accounts over the weekend, I've decided to change the rules of the Tarot Reading Giveaways. Head on over to the page for details; but if you want to skip to the fun part: I've changed from weekly winners to providing EVERYONE with a free reading!

I have yet to announce it, but know that there are future plans so it's not just a "one-time use" ;)

With the circumstances behind my lack of posting, I struggled to find appropriate articles to write. I have some Confessions written that I have been reluctant to post due to the sensitive and raw content... however remember that it's my story that I'm sharing, so why continue to hide it?

As a forewarning: I have written about being a victim of abuse. This is a hurdle for me to publish and share with others, but I am doing so for many reasons. It has taken me a long time to realize that the experiences added a lot to my character in positive ways, while negatively affecting me in many ways; also contributing to my late development as an intuitive empath.

I have also written to shed light on the isolation involved, especially as I am one that felt safer going into hiding and not pressing charges. A lot of people make quick judgments about accusations and stories that have surfaced years after the fact, and I want to share my story to explain (some reasons) why that happens.

While not related to being an empath, I want to share my recovery story for anyone else out there that's been a victim of abuse. Possibly more than anything though, it's noted that this will be the first time I share one particular secret outside closed doors. It was extremely difficult to write - so much so that I couldn't focus on the screen at times... and this is when I realized I can still do some recovery work by talking about it.

The revamp of the site has been delayed due to the drastic change involved. As I intend to gear this to become more of a "business" for me, I'm well aware that the whole site needs to change. I promise this will become easier to look at as it will become simpler to navigate, and posts (one per page) will be organized by topic.

If anyone has any feedback, feel free to contact me through the social media accounts or email me (see About Me for link)!

See you again soon! :)
