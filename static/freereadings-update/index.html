Tarot Readings Update!

So in the spirit of working towards some new and great things, I've decided to offer more when it comes to my Free Tarot Readings offering -- this will now be a MONTHLY service! This is at no charge to subscribers, so feel free to sign up if you haven't done so yet.

After becoming familiar with different tarot decks, and providing meaningful readings to myself and friends, I am confident enough to start offering readings as a paid service. Rest assured that my FREE readings are still insightful! I will however offer more extensive and in-depth readings through my paid services.

As we gear up towards yet another new year, and a time of settling a goal or two, I will be offering a form of reading known as a "prediction for the year" to come. This reading is a card for each month of the year, plus a 13th card - the overall theme for the year. I will be offering this service at a special price in time for the holidays, but will also offer a discount code to subscribers!

On December 31st, I will draw a name from the Subscribers list to win one of these readings as well!
